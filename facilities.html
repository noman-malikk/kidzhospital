Facilities and services

Every department presented with warmth, clarity, and premium structure.

This page avoids the usual dull hospital list. Each service is framed to help parents understand what it is for, why it matters, and how it supports a smoother pediatric care journey.

Critical care and stay

Hospital spaces designed for treatment, observation, and family reassurance.

These services shape how the hospital feels during admission, urgent care, neonatal care, and recovery.

Wards

Comfortable inpatient wards that support pediatric monitoring, nursing care, and a less intimidating stay.

  • Designed for children who need admission, observation, or extended treatment.
  • Prioritizes cleanliness, calmer lighting, and easier parent movement around the room.
  • Supports family confidence by feeling organized rather than overstimulating.

Private Rooms

Premium rooms for families who prefer more privacy, quieter surroundings, and added comfort during admission.

  • Ideal when parents want a more personal space for rest and communication.
  • Supports a more contained environment for recovery and overnight stays.
  • Complements the premium tone of the hospital’s wider brand identity.

NICU

Dedicated neonatal intensive care for newborns requiring specialist monitoring and closer clinical support.

  • Designed to communicate precision and reassurance rather than visual coldness.
  • Supports close observation of vulnerable newborns with controlled care pathways.
  • Parents benefit from clearer context and a more guided experience around critical care.

Nursery

Newborn nursery spaces that feel softer and more welcoming for early observation and support.

  • Useful for routine newborn monitoring and post-delivery transitional care.
  • Designed to lower stress at an especially sensitive stage for families.
  • Balances gentle presentation with a clear clinical purpose.

Children Emergency

Urgent pediatric care pathways for situations that require faster triage, evaluation, and treatment decisions.

  • Communicates readiness for urgent pediatric support in a clear, direct way.
  • Helps parents understand where to turn first when symptoms need quick attention.
  • Fits naturally into the hospital’s trust-building and conversion flow.

Children ICU

High-dependency pediatric intensive care for children who require closer monitoring and more advanced support.

  • Signals serious capability while preserving a composed visual tone for families.
  • Supports the hospital’s image as a full-service pediatric destination.
  • Works alongside emergency, inpatient, and diagnostic departments in a connected journey.
Diagnostics and support

Fast, dependable support services that keep care moving.

Labs

Diagnostic testing that supports quicker answers and a more connected treatment process.

  • Parents can understand why tests are needed and what happens next.
  • Works well in a hospital model that values responsiveness and reduced friction.
  • Helps avoid the fragmented feel families often experience elsewhere.

Pharmacy

On-site medication support that makes the care journey easier and more convenient for families.

  • Reduces unnecessary extra stops after consultation or discharge.
  • Supports better continuity between diagnosis, treatment, and parent instructions.
  • Strengthens the all-in-one hospital positioning on the website.

Ultrasound

Imaging support presented in a way that feels calm, modern, and easy for parents to understand.

  • Fits neatly into the broader diagnostics story on the site.
  • Useful for consultations that need additional imaging clarity.
  • Presented with a more reassuring visual tone than typical medical pages.

X-Ray

Practical imaging support for pediatric evaluation with clear layout and purposeful information hierarchy.

  • Supports a faster diagnostic pathway when timing matters.
  • Helps parents see that essential imaging services are close at hand.
  • Maintains the premium visual consistency of the rest of the website.
Designed around parents too
