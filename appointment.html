What happens next

Simple steps for families

The page is designed to reduce uncertainty by showing what parents can expect after sending a request.

1. Send the request

Parents enter the child’s details, preferred timing, and the relevant department.

2. Team receives the inquiry

With Formspree connected, the submission arrives by email at contact@kidzhospital.org.

3. Confirmation is shared

The hospital team can confirm timing, ask follow-up questions, or guide the family to the right desk.

Need faster help? Use the phone and emergency placeholders on the contact page for urgent situations.
